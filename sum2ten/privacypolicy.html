Privacy Policy

Sum 2 Ten is committed to protecting the privacy of our young users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our app. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. We do not share, sell, or rent any user data to third parties. Our app may use third-party services for analytics or advertising, which adhere to strict privacy standards and are designed to be COPPA compliant. By using Sum 2 Ten, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. We encourage parents and guardians to review this policy and supervise their children's online activities.
